ABOUT ME

I am actually studying a CFGS in 3D animations, Games and Interactive Enviornmets at Teknós/UVIC, i took the Artistic "Batxillerat" at EART Vic and the "ESO" at IES Vic.

I started learning Blender and a lot of its functions by myself, self taught, because i wanted to create Skyrim mods. Learning Blender helped me a lot down the road for optimizing my workflow in certain projects, though i actually use Maya as my main 3d modelling software tool, even though i won't doubt in using Blender in certain cases, i also use Zbrush for creating characters, some props or detailing some models.

During my free time i like to try and learn new things related to 3D modellg and sculpting, and then apply what i learnt to my workflow.

El què m'agrada més és el Modelatge, Sculpting i Texturització, també tinc coneixements bàsics sobre disseny, il·luminació, anatomia humana artística i animació. What i like the most is Modelling, Sculpting and Texturing, i also have some knowledge in design, illumination, artistic human anatomy and animation.
I also have basic knowledge about HTML and CSS with whom i created this portfolio.
